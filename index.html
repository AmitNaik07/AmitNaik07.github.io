Intro

I am currently working at Humana as a Cognitive/Machine Learning Professional 2. I finished my Masters Degree in Computer Science in May 2019. I interned at Humana in summer of 2018 as a Data Analyst.

I completed my undergrad in India from Pune Institute of Computer Technology. I worked as web designer for a startup company called Tweeny in my 3rd year. This gave me some experience in working with AngularJS and MongoDB for a full stack project.

Our final year project won the second place prize for the best Networking domain project in our college tech fest (Impetus and Concepts, INC). We also came second in the Paper Presentation competition hosted by BITS Goa.

Started a project with my friend Abhinuv Pitale which snowballed into an idea for a startup. We received funding from our college to give us steady platform to kickstart our campaign. It's coming soon, so just Hold On.

About Me

I was born in India and then moved to the US six months later. I did my schooling there in Cedar Rapids, Iowa till 5th grade, after which I shifted back to India. I would say that the next 11 years of my life were probably the most defining. My education in India and life experiences taught me how to look at things in a different perspective.

My father was always highly involved in the IT industry and I learnt the basics of computer science at a very young age. I started dabbling with webpages at the age of 11. After my shifted to India, I worked on Java during my school days and C++ in High School. I realized that my true interests lie in Databases and dealing with Big Data when I had taken that course in my Undergrad.

I was influenced by my father who guided me in this particular field which developed my interests even further. As Machine Learning and Data Analytics gained momentum in the tech industry, this opened a whole new avenue for me to explore.

Working on projects in this field fueled my ambition to learn more about this domain and this compelled me to do my Masters. I was back in the US after almost exactly 11 years! It was pretty daunting at first but with the right company and oppurtunities I would say it's been a wonderful experience. I learnt a lot, not just technically but also those two years helped me improve my overall personality.

I am a heavy book reader and would love to sit in a corner and read all day. Also, a big sports fanatic, with tennis and table tennis being a few of my top favorite sports to play. I am always excited to work on new and different things which will help me improve my overall skill set.
